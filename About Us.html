ABOUT

Hyciine

Hyciine is a thoughtfully crafted body-oil brand dedicated to simple elegance and everyday care. Our lightweight, beautifully scented oils nourish skin with a soft, lasting glow, while our signature glass packaging brings a touch of understated luxury to your routine. Founded by a family of believers, we honor values of quality, honesty, and care—creating products that feel uplifting and graceful without the fuss.

Founded in Abuja, Hyciine was created with a simple idea: body care can be both luxurious and kind to the earth. Every detail—from our recyclable glass bottles to our sustainably sourced ingredients—is designed to be eco-friendly without sacrificing beauty or quality. Hyciine is where graceful packaging meets clean, nourishing formulas, offering a touch of everyday elegance with scents you’ll love to wear.
